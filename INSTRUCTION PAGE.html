INSTRUCTIONS FOR FILLING APPLICATION

1. Before filling up the form candidates are advised to carefully go through the Relevant Notification published in the Leading News Papers and also available in the “ Notifications/Advertisements” in Candidate segment page.

2. Click on “Apply” button it will open option "Candidate Registration".

3. For Part-I Candidate Registration process you have to click on “Candidate Registration” option it will fetch out Candidate Basic Registration page in which the candidates have to fill fundamental information, particulars and details asked for. Fields with * indication are mandatory and essential to be filled in by the candidate. Every field has clear instructions for filling up online form are written, which should be carefully read and strictly followed by the candidates while filling up the form.

4. Please keep the pdf/doc of the following documents with you while filling the form

          Adhaar Card (Front and Back)
          Pan Card(Front and Back)
          Photo ID(white Background)
          Signture
          Birth Certificate
          Leaving Certificate
          JEE/CET marksheet
